This project involved redesigning the brand identity of Manipal Hospital. It was observed that the current logotype and branding did not effectively convey a sense of comfort and safety. Therefore, we introduced a new look with a complete color and logo change to better align with these values.

The logo lacks a sense of strength due to the use of small letters, which does not convey the hospital's innovative and forward-looking nature. Additionally, the spacing below the logo detracts from its visual appeal, and the color scheme does not complement the typeface effectively. As a hospital focused on innovation and the future, the current branding fails to reflect these values.

About the project

The logo features a stretched "M" with intersecting elements, designed to resemble a DNA structure. This design symbolizes the universal connection among all humans, emphasizing the shared essence and unity inherent in humanity. The intersecting lines create a dynamic and visually engaging element, reinforcing the brand's commitment to innovation and forward-thinking. This thoughtful design choice not only enhances the logo's aesthetic appeal but also aligns with the brand's core values and mission.

Logo

The logo uses Venice Blue and Shocking Orange for the typeface, with Quill Grey as the background. Venice Blue symbolizes trust and professionalism, while Shocking Orange represents energy and innovation. Quill Grey provides a neutral backdrop, enhancing the visibility and impact of the vibrant colors. This combination effectively communicates the hospital's values of cleanliness and brightness.

The typography for Manipal Hospital utilizes the Minion Variable Concept Regular typeface. Designed by Robert Slimbach, this serif typeface is known for its versatility and readability. Stream Together: Enhancing Netflix with Binge Feature

Binge is a new feature on Netflix that allows users to add a friend to watch a movie together and communicate in real-time. Currently, the feature supports audio communication, but our team is working on adding video functionality, ensuring all design considerations are met. Binge is offered as a separate plan on Netflix, enhancing the shared viewing experience.

Project Overview

Introducing Binge, a groundbreaking feature on Netflix that allows users to watch movies simultaneously with a friend while communicating in real-time. Currently, Binge supports audio communication, with plans to integrate video functionality soon. This feature is offered as a separate plan, enhancing the shared viewing experience and bringing people closer together through entertainment.

Binge

The Binge logo is crafted to evoke the feelings of comfort and peace that viewers experience during screen time. The design features an inverted letter "i," symbolizing a unique perspective and innovation. The dot of the "i" is represented as a screen, reinforcing the connection to the viewing experience. This inversion not only adds a distinctive touch but also signifies the idea of looking at things from a different angle, aligning with the innovative nature of the Binge feature.

Aditya Menon . 2024

The logo's color palette includes white, Kelly green, pale canary, and tangerine red. White is used for the main text to ensure visibility. The green background symbolizes cleanliness and an environmental vibe, aligning with the company's focus on cleaning. Pale canary and tangerine red are used for the sun, representing bright sunshine and adding a subtle, uncluttered look to the logo typography. This combination of colors enhances the visual appeal and reinforces the brand's message of cleanliness and brightness.

Brand Logo (Finalized)

During the logo creation process, I meticulously developed four distinct concepts as initial drafts. These drafts were crafted to provide the client with a comprehensive understanding of the mood and direction of the design. By presenting a variety of creative approaches, I aimed to ensure that the client could visualize the potential visual identity of their brand from multiple perspectives. This thorough exploration allowed for a more informed decision-making process, ultimately leading to a logo that truly resonates with the client's vision and goals

Concept Logos

A2 Bright, a leading manufacturer of kitchen and floor cleaning liquids, is renowned for its strong market presence. Their motto, encapsulated in the wordmark "Bright," emphasizes the effectiveness of their products in achieving clean and shiny floors. By offering high-quality solutions, A2 Bright ensures superior cleanliness and shine, solidifying its reputation as a trusted name in the cleaning industry.
